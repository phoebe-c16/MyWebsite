This is a photo of the Great Barrier Reef, which is the biggest reef in the world, located in Australia. Due to global warming and climate change, the coral is suffering intense bleaching.

Half of the great barrier reef has been bleached to death since 2016. Mass coral bleaching, a global problem triggered by climate change, occurs when unnaturally hot ocean water results in colourful algae leaving the coral. This results in starvation of the coral as there mutualistic relationship with Algae is ruined.
